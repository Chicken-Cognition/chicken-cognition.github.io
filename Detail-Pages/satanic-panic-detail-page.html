Satanic Panic


Role on Project: Solo Developer

Software Used: Godot, Blender

Description:

Satanic Panic is my current project. I've worked on it for about 4 months in my spare time (as of August 2025). It's a Tower Defense FPS game inspired by "Plants Vs Zombies", and is about fending off demons while blasting rock music. It still has a long way to go before it's finished.

In the game, the player has to prevent demons from getting to the stage. They can place various machines on grids around the map that the demons pass through. After every wave the game gives the player 3 randomized choices for what happens in the next wave (See the middle screenshot on the left). Every 5 waves the player is given the option to escape, the level with all the money they earned.

My favorite feature I've added so far is the ability for the player to add custom music. The game creates a music folder next to the .exe, and will play any .mp3 or .wav files the player puts inside.
