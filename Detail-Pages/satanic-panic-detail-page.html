Satanic Panic


Role on Project: Solo Developer

Software Used: Godot, Blender

Description:

Satanic Panic is my current project. I've worked on it for about 4 months in my spare time (as of August 2025). It's a Tower Defense FPS game inspired by "Plants Vs Zombies", and is about fending off demons while blasting rock music. It still has a long way to go.

My favorite feature I've added so far is the ability for the player to add custom music. The game creates a music folder next to the .exe, and will play any .mp3 or .wav files the player puts inside.

I'm hoping to sell this game on steam when it's finished. I have high hopes, because all the playtesters so far have been asking me "Whens the next update?", and "What are you adding next?".
