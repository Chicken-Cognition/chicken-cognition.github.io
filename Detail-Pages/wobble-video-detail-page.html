Wobble Video


Role on Project: 3D Artist

Software Used: Blender, Adobe Premiere, Krita

Description:

The Wobble Delivery is a video my brother, and I made together. I only put things that I exclusively made myself on this page, but I had a hand in many other scenes in the video.

This is a non-game project, but I still wanted to put it here because of how many skills I learned that apply to games. In particular, things like how to frame a scene, post processing, and how to think creatively to pull off some of the visuals.
