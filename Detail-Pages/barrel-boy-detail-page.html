Barrel Boy


Role on Project: Solo Developer

Software Used: Unreal Engine, Blender

Description:

Barrel Boy was my "Dream Game" idea I had before I had started learning how to make games. These days I realise how overscoped it was, but I still did some work on it that I'm very proud of, and want to show off.

There are lot of things about the original idea I would like to revisit. Like the kettle town, and rubber hose infection in particular. However, It'll have to become something much easier to make than a big RPG, and 3D platformer combo with multiple levels to explore.
