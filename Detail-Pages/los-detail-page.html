Light Out of Sight

Role on Project: Solo Developer

Software Used: Godot, Blender, Substance Painter, Material Maker, Krita

Description:

Light Out of Sight is my first major project that I've finished. It took me about 2 years to make. I don't consider it a "complete" game, but I learned so much from it that I've brought to every project I've worked on since.

The only things I didn't create myself are the music, a couple models, and one set of textures. Everything else I made myself.
